Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their data associated with the Emili application (“the App”).

How to request data deletion

  1. Send an email to: hind@emili.net
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request — Emili
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • Your full name
    • The email address used with the App (if applicable)
    • The platform you used (Facebook / Instagram)
    • Any relevant profile/page identifier (if applicable)

What data will be deleted

Upon verifying the request, we will delete any data associated with your account that was collected through the App, such as authentication identifiers and any stored settings related to your usage.

Processing time

We process deletion requests as soon as possible. Typical processing time is within 7 business days.

Need help?

If you have questions about this process, contact us at hind@emili.net.
